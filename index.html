Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our appliance repair services in Guelph

How quickly can you repair my appliance? +
We offer same-day service for most appliance repairs in Guelph. When you call before noon, we can typically schedule a technician to arrive the same day. For emergency situations like a broken refrigerator or commercial equipment failure, we prioritize your repair and often complete the work within hours. Most standard repairs are completed in a single visit since our technicians carry a comprehensive inventory of common parts.
Do you repair all appliance brands? +
Yes, our certified technicians are trained and authorized to repair all major appliance brands including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Frigidaire, Maytag, KitchenAid, Bosch, Electrolux, and many more. We have access to genuine manufacturer parts for all brands we service and stay current on the latest repair techniques through ongoing factory training programs.
What is your service call fee? +
Our standard service call fee is $79, which includes a complete diagnostic assessment of your appliance. If you proceed with the repair, this fee is credited toward your total repair cost. We provide upfront pricing for all repairs before starting any work, so you'll know the exact cost with no hidden fees or surprises. Senior discounts and military discounts are available upon request.
Do you offer a warranty on repairs? +
Absolutely! All our repairs come with a comprehensive 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. This means if the same problem occurs within 90 days of your repair, we'll fix it again at no additional charge. We use only genuine manufacturer-approved parts which often carry their own extended warranties. This warranty coverage gives you complete peace of mind that your repair will last.
Should I repair or replace my appliance? +
This depends on several factors including the age of your appliance, the cost of repair, and the appliance's overall condition. As a general rule, if your appliance is less than halfway through its expected lifespan and the repair cost is less than 50% of replacement cost, repair is usually the better choice. Our technicians will provide an honest assessment and help you make an informed decision without pressure to choose either option.
Do you service commercial kitchen equipment? +
Yes, we specialize in commercial kitchen appliance repair for restaurants, cafes, hotels, and institutional kitchens throughout Guelph. We understand that equipment downtime directly impacts your business, so we offer priority scheduling, after-hours service, and emergency repair availability. Our technicians have specialized training in commercial-grade equipment and carry commercial parts for faster repairs.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major payment methods for your convenience including cash, debit cards, Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and e-transfer. Payment is due upon completion of service. For commercial clients, we offer NET 30 payment terms with approved credit. We also provide detailed itemized invoices for insurance claims or warranty reimbursement if needed.
Are your technicians licensed and insured? +
Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. They undergo comprehensive background checks before joining our team and maintain current certifications through ongoing factory training. We carry full liability insurance and workers compensation coverage for your complete protection. Our technicians also arrive in clearly marked vehicles and wear company uniforms with proper identification.
